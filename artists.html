Abel Ghekiere

Abel Ghekiere makes a beautiful mix of jazz, ambient, folk and field recordings. Telling a story of grief and consolation, the music is magical both live and on the album. Their debut album “Voor het verdwijnt, en daarna” was released on Rotkat Records in 2021. Live Abel Ghekiere surrounds himself with Tobias Volckaert (saxophone), Ana van Liedekerke (cello), Maarten Joostens (gitare) en Orlan Ghekiere (drums). On the 16th of march 2019 they played at OVERLAST.
